About.

¡Hola! My name is Jaime Santos Rodríguez, I'm a Postdoctoral Fellow at the MPIM Bonn , my mentor there is Prof. Dr. K.-T. Sturm.

Previously I was a Ph.D. student at the Universidad Autónoma de Madrid. and a member of the ICMAT. My adviser was Prof. Luis Guijarro.

Research.

I’m interested mostly in the areas of Metric Geometry and Riemannian geometry, however my particular research topics often deal as well with Geometric Measure Theory, Isometric group actions and Optimal Transport.

I’m particularly interested in metric measure spaces that admit generalizations of lower curvature bounds (Sectional and Ricci). These spaces appear naturally as Gromov-Hausdorff limits of manifolds. My research focuses on their isometry groups and the effect of isometric Lie group actions on these spaces.
